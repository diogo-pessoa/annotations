## RCA Template (Why / What / How)

### **Why** *(Impact + Trigger)*

* Why is this incident a problem?
* What user/system impact occurred?
* What triggered the investigation?



### **What** *(Cause)*

* What actually failed?
* What was the root cause (not the symptom)?
* What conditions allowed it to happen?



### **How** *(Fix + Prevention)*

* How was it mitigated or fixed?
* How will we prevent recurrence?
* How will we detect this earlier next time?
